Frequently asked questions

Our metal core with ceramic coating resists scratches and impact from typical daily use. A soft base protects countertops. Hand-wash recommended to preserve finish.

Our universal adapter accommodates most 28mm and 410-thread pumps and caps. See the compatibility guide for brand-specific fits.

Refill pouches are curbside-recyclable where facilities exist; our plant-based liners are industrial-compostable. Check local guidelines for best disposal practices.
